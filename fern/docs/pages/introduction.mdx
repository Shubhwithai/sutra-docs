# Get Started with SUTRA™

**SUTRA™** is a family of multilingual large language models (LMLMs) developed by **TWO AI**.It is built with a powerful **dual-transformer architecture** that combines **Mixture of Experts (MoE)** and **dense transformer layers**, enabling highly efficient performance across more than **50 global and regional languages**.

SUTRA is designed to serve developers, data scientists, and enterprise teams looking for advanced language intelligence that scales—from **rapid prototyping** to **full-scale production**.

## 🔑 Key Capabilities

## **SUTRA models offer:**
### 🌐 Multilingual Mastery  
Native-level fluency across 50+ languages including **Hindi**, **Bengali**, **Gujarati**, **Tamil**, **Telugu**, **Japanese**, **Korean**, and more.

### ⚡ High Throughput  
Efficient inference with **low latency** for real-time use cases.

### 📦 Structured Output Support  
Return **JSON**, **key-value pairs**, and task-specific formats via **prompt engineering**.

### 🧠 Advanced Reasoning  
Handle complex prompts requiring **logic**, **step-by-step computation**, and **multi-turn memory**.
